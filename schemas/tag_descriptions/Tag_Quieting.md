Use Tag Quieting to silence specific tags by EPC ID. Quieted tags do not respond during inventory rounds, reducing unwanted reads and improving accuracy in high-density environments.

**Key Features:**
- **Selective Quieting**: Quiet only targeted EPCs without affecting other tags
- **Bidirectional Control**: Use `quiet` to suppress and `unquiet` to restore
- **Persistent State**: Quieted tags remain silent until explicitly unquieted
